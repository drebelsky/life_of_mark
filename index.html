Life of Mark

About

"Life of Mark" is a short interactive piece of fiction I wrote as my final project for an English class in high school.

Instructions

Start by clicking on this link. After that, more or less, just follow the onscreen instructions. Note that sometimes to advance the story, you will have to click on a link, and further, note that the project isn't done until the acknowledgments page (there is a semi-long black screen that may otherwise be confusing).
